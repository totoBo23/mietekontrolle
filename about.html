About Us

MietKontrolle.at helps tenants in Innsbruck assess possible rent overpayment through a clear and structured process.

We combine a fast digital rent check with case pre-review and next-step support together with specialized legal partners.

What we do

A clear and professional approach to rent review

Many tenants are unsure whether their rent is legally compliant. At the same time, it is often difficult to judge which facts matter and what the right next step should be. That is exactly where our service is meant to help.

We start with a quick first estimate. If there are signs of possible overpayment, the case moves into a more structured review together with specialized legal partners.

How we work

How the process works

1

First estimate

The calculator provides a quick first orientation based on the key information entered by the tenant.

2

Case review

If the case looks suitable, our specialized legal partners review the contract and rent data in more detail.

3

Next steps

We come back to you with the next steps to recover the money. Where possible, the first goal is a fair out-of-court solution.

Costs and risk

No financial risk for tenants

We cover the costs of pursuing the recovery of your money and moving the case forward. We only receive payment if money is successfully recovered for you. Our compensation is then a percentage of the amount that has been recovered.
